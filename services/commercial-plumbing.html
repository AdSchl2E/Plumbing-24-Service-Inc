The frequency of grease trap cleaning depends on the size of the trap and your business's cooking volume. Generally, most restaurants should have their grease traps cleaned every 1-3 months. High-volume establishments may require monthly cleaning, while smaller operations might only need quarterly service. In Fort Myers, local regulations often require regular maintenance and documentation of grease trap cleaning. We can help establish a cleaning schedule that keeps you compliant with local codes while preventing costly sewer backups.

Backflow testing is a procedure to ensure that your backflow prevention device is working properly. These devices prevent contaminated water from flowing backward into the clean public water supply. Commercial properties in Miami are typically required by local regulations to have their backflow preventers tested annually.

This testing is mandatory because it protects public health by preventing potential contamination of the municipal water system. Our licensed technicians can perform this required annual testing, provide necessary documentation for compliance, and make repairs if your device fails the test.
