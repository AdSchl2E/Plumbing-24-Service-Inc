Expert Pipe Repair & Replacement

Professional solutions for all pipe problems in Miami homes and businesses

When pipes leak, burst, or corrode, they can cause significant damage to your property and disrupt your daily life. At Plumbing 24 Service Inc, we provide fast, reliable pipe repair and replacement services to restore your plumbing system's functionality and protect your Miami home or business.

Our licensed plumbers are equipped with the latest tools and technology to diagnose pipe issues accurately and implement effective solutions. We handle all types of residential and commercial pipe problems, from minor leaks to major replacements, ensuring minimal disruption to your property.

Common Pipe Problems We Fix

Our experienced technicians address all types of pipe issues for Miami residents, including:

  • Leaking pipes in walls, ceilings, floors, or under slabs
  • Burst pipes causing water damage and flooding
  • Corroded pipes leading to water discoloration or reduced flow
  • Frozen pipes that have cracked or damaged connections
  • Root intrusions causing repeated sewer line backups
  • Incorrectly installed pipes that cause ongoing issues

Whether you're dealing with a minor leak or need a complete repiping of your Miami property, our team has the expertise and equipment to provide efficient, long-lasting solutions that fit your budget and timeline.

Don't ignore these warning signs of pipe problems in your Miami home

Visible Water Leaks

Water stains on ceilings, walls, or floors often indicate a hidden pipe leak that requires immediate attention. Even small drips can cause significant damage over time and lead to mold growth.

Decreased Water Pressure

A sudden or gradual drop in water pressure throughout your home or in specific fixtures may indicate pipe corrosion, leaks, or mineral buildup that's restricting water flow.

Discolored Water

4

An unexpected increase in your water bill without corresponding usage could indicate a hidden leak somewhere in your plumbing system that's wasting water and costing you money.

5

Unpleasant Odors

Persistent sewage or musty smells inside your Miami home often indicate a broken sewer pipe, vent pipe issue, or water damage from a hidden leak that's promoting mold growth.

Pipe Repair FAQ

Common questions about our pipe repair and replacement services

Signs of a pipe leaking inside a wall include: water stains or discoloration on walls or ceilings, a musty smell in a specific area, warped or bubbling paint or wallpaper, mold growth, warm spots on the wall (hot water pipe), or consistently wet flooring along the wall. We use specialized moisture meters and thermal imaging cameras to detect hidden leaks without unnecessary wall damage.

Pipe repair costs in Miami vary based on the pipe location, material, accessibility, and repair method. Simple repairs might range from $150-$350, while more complex issues involving wall access or slab leaks can range from $500-$2,000+. Complete repiping projects for homes typically start around $4,000-$8,000 depending on home size and pipe material. We always provide detailed upfront estimates after properly assessing the situation.

Pipe replacement is generally recommended when: pipes are extensively corroded (especially galvanized steel pipes over 40-50 years old), you have recurring leaks in the same plumbing system, pipes are made of problematic materials like polybutylene, there's severe mineral buildup restricting flow, or when repairs would cost more than 30-50% of replacement. Our technicians will assess your specific situation and recommend the most cost-effective long-term solution.

Common causes of pipe leaks or bursts include:

  • Corrosion due to age, water chemistry, or galvanic reactions
  • High water pressure exceeding pipe specifications
  • Freezing temperatures causing water expansion inside pipes
  • Shifting soil or foundation movement stressing pipes
  • Tree root intrusion into underground pipes
  • Improper installation or use of incompatible materials
  • Physical damage during renovations or construction

Regular plumbing inspections can identify potential issues before they lead to catastrophic failures.

Trenchless pipe repair is a modern technique that allows us to repair or replace underground pipes with minimal excavation and property disruption. The two main methods we use are:

  • Pipe lining - Installing a resin-coated liner inside the damaged pipe that hardens to create a new pipe within the old one
  • Pipe bursting - Pulling a new pipe through the existing pipe while simultaneously fracturing the old pipe outward

These techniques save time, money, and prevent landscape damage compared to traditional excavation methods. However, they're not suitable for all situations, so our technicians will determine if your pipe issue is a good candidate for trenchless repair.
